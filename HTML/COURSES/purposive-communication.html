ABOUT THE SUBJECT

Purposive Communication develops students' ability to communicate effectively in various contexts using different modes and strategies. The course emphasizes critical thinking, cultural sensitivity, and ethical communication practices.

Students learn to create clear, coherent, and compelling messages across different media platforms while considering audience, purpose, and context in their communication endeavors.
