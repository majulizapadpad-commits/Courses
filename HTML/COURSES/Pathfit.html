ABOUT THE SUBJECT

PathFit (Physical Activities Towards Health and Fitness) promotes physical wellness through various sports and fitness activities. The course emphasizes the importance of regular exercise and healthy lifestyle choices.

Students engage in different physical activities, learn about nutrition, and develop personal fitness plans to maintain lifelong health and wellness habits.
