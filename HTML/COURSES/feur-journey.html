ABOUT THE SUBJECT

FEUR Journey is an orientation course that introduces students to the university's culture, values, and academic expectations. It helps new students transition smoothly into university life and understand the FEUR community.

The course covers university history, student services, academic policies, and personal development strategies to ensure student success throughout their academic journey at FEUR.
