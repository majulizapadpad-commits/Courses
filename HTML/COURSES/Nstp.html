ABOUT THE SUBJECT

National Service Training Program (NSTP) develops students' civic consciousness and commitment to community service. The program aims to enhance social responsibility and promote national unity through various community-based activities.

Students participate in community outreach programs, environmental projects, and social awareness campaigns that contribute to nation-building and character development.
